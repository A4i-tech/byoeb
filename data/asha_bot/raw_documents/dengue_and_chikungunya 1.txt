Timely diagnosis, treatment and precautions which can save you from Dengue and Chikungunya:

What are dengue and chikungunya?
Dengue and chikungunya are vector borne diseases which are spread by the Aedes mosquito and these mosquitoes are produced in the stagnant clean water and generally bites during the day time. Wear full sleeve clothes specially during the rainy season and avoid collecting water, maintain cleanliness near houses, empty the water of the water cooler once a week, then dry it and refill with fresh water. Use mosquito nets while sleeping in the daytime as well. 

What are the symptoms of dengue?
High fever, frontal headache, especially behind the eyes, pain in muscles, bleeding from nose and gums, red rashes on the body.  

How to treat dengue?
If symptoms occur, go to the government clinical facilities and get the diagnosis free of cost. Upon diagnosis, if tests for dengue or chikungunya come out to be positive, then go take the proper medication prescribed by the doctor. 

What are the Symptoms of Chikungunya? 
High fever, headache, abnormal joint pain, red rashes on the body. 

How to prevent Dengue and Chikungunya?
Keep the utensils in which water is stored covered, do not collect water in the cooler, drum, bucket, tank if not required, always let them dry properly before using it again. Do not let the water be collected near the hand pump/taps as well. If the water is collected on any floor/ground, pour kerosine oil or engine oil on it. Maintain cleanliness nearby, use mosquito net while sleeping, wear full sleeves clothes.
