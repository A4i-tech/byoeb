Know Your Child’s Immunization Schedule (India)

Row-by-row schedule (each line is one vaccine entry with age, vaccine, route, and notes).

AT BIRTH
- Age: At Birth | Vaccine: BCG | Route: Injection (upper arm) | Notes: Single dose; protects against tuberculosis
- Age: At Birth | Vaccine: OPV – 0 dose | Route: Oral | Notes: First OPV dose; next at 6w, 10w, 14w; protects against polio
- Age: At Birth | Vaccine: Hepatitis B (birth dose) | Route: Injection | Notes: Single dose; protects against hepatitis B

6 WEEKS
- Age: 6 Weeks | Vaccine: OPV – 1 | Route: Oral | Notes: Second OPV dose; next at 10w & 14w
- Age: 6 Weeks | Vaccine: Pentavalent – 1 | Route: Injection | Notes: 1st of 3 doses (6w, 10w, 14w); protects Diphtheria, Pertussis, Tetanus, Hep B, Hib
- Age: 6 Weeks | Vaccine: RVV – 1 (Rotavirus) | Route: Oral | Notes: 1st of 3 doses (6w, 10w, 14w); protects against rotavirus diarrhea
- Age: 6 Weeks | Vaccine: PCV – 1* | Route: Injection | Notes: 1st of 2 doses; 2nd at 14w; protects against infections like pneumonia/meningitis/septicemia
- Age: 6 Weeks | Vaccine: fIPV – 1 | Route: Injection | Notes: 1st of 2 doses; 2nd at 14w; protects against polio

10 WEEKS
- Age: 10 Weeks | Vaccine: Pentavalent – 2 | Route: Injection | Notes: 2nd dose; last at 14w
- Age: 10 Weeks | Vaccine: OPV – 2 | Route: Oral | Notes: 3rd OPV dose; last at 14w
- Age: 10 Weeks | Vaccine: RVV – 2 (Rotavirus) | Route: Oral | Notes: 2nd dose; last at 14w

14 WEEKS
- Age: 14 Weeks | Vaccine: Pentavalent – 3 | Route: Injection | Notes: Final pentavalent dose
- Age: 14 Weeks | Vaccine: OPV – 3 | Route: Oral | Notes: Final primary OPV dose
- Age: 14 Weeks | Vaccine: RVV – 3 (Rotavirus) | Route: Oral | Notes: Final rotavirus dose
- Age: 14 Weeks | Vaccine: PCV – 2 | Route: Injection | Notes: 2nd PCV dose
- Age: 14 Weeks | Vaccine: fIPV – 2 | Route: Injection | Notes: Final fIPV dose

9–12 MONTHS
- Age: 9–12 Months | Vaccine: MR – 1 (Measles & Rubella) | Route: Injection | Notes: 1st of 2 doses; 2nd at 16–24 months
- Age: 9–12 Months | Vaccine: JE – 1** | Route: Injection | Notes: 1st of 2 doses; 2nd at 16–24 months
- Age: 9–12 Months | Vaccine: PCV Booster* | Route: Injection | Notes: Single booster dose

16–24 MONTHS
- Age: 16–24 Months | Vaccine: MR – 2 | Route: Injection | Notes: 2nd MR dose
- Age: 16–24 Months | Vaccine: JE – 2** | Route: Injection | Notes: Final JE dose
- Age: 16–24 Months | Vaccine: DPT Booster – 1 | Route: Injection | Notes: 1st of 2 boosters; 2nd at 5–6 years
- Age: 16–24 Months | Vaccine: OPV Booster | Route: Oral | Notes: Single booster dose

5–6 YEARS
- Age: 5–6 Years | Vaccine: DPT Booster – 2 | Route: Injection | Notes: 2nd DPT booster dose

10 YEARS
- Age: 10 Years | Vaccine: Td (Tetanus & adult Diphtheria) | Route: Injection | Notes: Single dose

16 YEARS
- Age: 16 Years | Vaccine: Td (Tetanus & adult Diphtheria) | Route: Injection | Notes: Single dose

Notes
- * PCV schedule may be shown with asterisks in some references (as in the source table).
- ** JE vaccines are marked with double-asterisks in the source table.
